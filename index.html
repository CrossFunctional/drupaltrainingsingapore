13 - 18 of June • Singapore • Drupal Introduction & Training

Come learn Drupal!

Register

Learn how to succeed with Drupal!

Drupal is the open source CMS powering the web today, from governments and universities to small businesses and enterprises. If you're interested in learning more about this open source software, or looking to increase your existing knowledge and skills, then come join us from June 13th - 18th.

While the Drupal Developer Network (Singapore) RoS is putting on their 3rd annual DrupalCamp, we will also be holding an informational session and additional training courses. These events will bring international and local expertise with Drupal to the vibrance of Singapore. Learn how Drupal is driving results for organisations across the globe and get experience implementing Drupal for your own goals.

Events

  • Hello Drupal!
    Fri, 13 June
    Register (Free!)

    Come enjoy a relaxing and informative Friday afternoon with a guided tour by Drupal veterans.
    Get introduced to Drupal and see its capabilities to address your digital needs. You will get a chance to see behind the curtain into two (2) real world examples and how they delivered high impact results. There will be ample time to get your questions answered by experts. Afterwards, everyone is invited for some business networking over drinks.

     

    The event is designed for people considering using Drupal.

  • DrupalCamp Singapore
    Sat, 14 June
    Register

    DrupalCamp Singapore is an annual conference that brings together web developers around Southeast Asia to learn, share and explore web development and the Drupal content management system. Entering its 3rd year in 2014, the conference has attracted over 300 attendees from across Asia to gather in Singapore since 2012. This year, the focus will be on Drupal 8 and how it will impact the web industry greatly, featuring speakers & attendees from Australia, Philippines, Indonesia, Singapore etc.

  • Training: Drupal in a Day
    Mon, 16 June
    Register

    Get a solid introduction to Drupal 7 in one day. Learn about the most essential features and concepts of Drupal through hands on activities. By the end of this day you will be familiar with Drupal terminology and be able to identify how Drupal sites are constructed. You will know how to identify and choose modules to get the functionality you need.
